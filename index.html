Professional & EXPERIENCED Hanford Plumbers Serving the Central Valley


Welcome to Alan Laird Plumbing ("ALP"). We are local Hanford plumbers dedicated to helping you with your plumbing needs and will assist you with honesty, quality and great customer service.

Centrally located in Kings County, Alan Laird Plumbing is proud to provide general engineering and plumbing services to California's Central Valley and beyond.  Whether its residential, commercial or agricultural, ALP has the know-how, experience and equipment to get your job done.

ALP's services include, but are not limited to, commercial and residential new construction, remodels, additions, agricultural work, excavation and site work.  We also offer emergency plumbing services 24 hours a day, 7 days a week.

Our team of experts are here to help you through every step of your plumbing project by taking away your stress and making it an enjoyable and easy process for you. Our commitment to quality is only matched by our dedication to your complete satisfaction.

Thank you for visiting our website and we look forward to receiving your call today for a free work estimate.
